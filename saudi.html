Saudi Prometric

The Saudi Commission for Health Specialties (SCFHS) mandates that healthcare professionals intend to work in Saudi Arabia. The Dataflow procedure undergoes Dataflow(Verification), Registration( Credential) and Exam registration. This process, managed by the Saudi Data Flow Group, involves verifying the authenticity of educational degrees, work experience, and professional licenses. We deliver exceptional support for data flow processes, guaranteeing smooth operations and accurate documentation verification for our customers.
